Welcome to the 22nd Annual Felis Cat Agility Tournament.

This tournament has been held every year since 1999 to test the speed, coordination and discipline of cats all around the world. Furthermore, the skills tested in this tournament demonstrates the quality and depth of the relationship between the cats and their owners. The cats must complete a course in under 270 seconds, completing each course in the prescribed manner. Lures such as lasers and toys may be used, however food treats are not permitted. The obstacles are arranged in a circle which includes tunnels, steps and hurdles. For a typical contest, there are between 6 and 14 obstacles.

Anyone can join!
Location: Al-Rayyan Stadium, Al-Rayyan, Qatar
Date: 27th December 2022
Time: 8am-5pm
Prize: A month's worth of POWERCAT products
